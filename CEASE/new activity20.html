After waking up


Brush your teeth right away, change your morning routine. if you usually shower first and then have breakfast, switch it up and have breakfast first .Eat your breakfast in a different place in your home and take your dog for a walk.




After meals

You can brush your teeth, Read a book or newspaper, Go for a long walk outdoors or call a friend.




When bored

Draw, write, or do a crossword puzzle to keep your hands busy.




When Driving

Take a different route than usual to avoid places where you would normally light up




During Breaks

Avoid your old break room and take your break in a no-smoking area with non-smokers




When watching Tv

Post a reminder notice on the TV and watch Tv for shorter periods of time.




When stressed

Remind yourself that smoking will not take away stress or anger. Try deep breathing or practice relaxation techniques. Talk to a friend or get professional help from a counsellor. You can also exercise or take a walk.




When Socializing

Avoid other smokers whenever possible; avoid public places where smoking is allowed. Ask others around you not to smoke. spend more time with non-smokers.




When watching weight

Exercise regularly, remember to always take the stairs instead of the elevator and to park farther away from store entrances

It's important to recognize the activities, times of the day, and emotions that might make you want to smoke.
